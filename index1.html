Super Saiyan

Super Saiyan is a powerful, iconic transformation in the Dragon Ball series, first achieved by Goku in 1991. Triggered by intense emotion (rage or need), it boosts a Saiyan's strength, speed, and Ki, turning hair gold and eyes green/blue. It is an evolution beyond the base form, with levels including SSJ2, SSJ3, and divine forms like Super Saiyan God.
